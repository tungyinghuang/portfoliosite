Scerf, the smart shopping cart

Focus: UX research, UX/UI design, Prototyping

Skills: Qualitative research, Persona, Customer Journey Map, Competitive Analysis, User flow, Evaluation

It's a UX/UI project of Choicony Electronic Inc. Intership program. It's a new type of shopping cart combined with automatic following car body and mobile tablet with four main functions, especially including corporate social responsibility, which can provide welfare service during customers’ shopping behavior. Connecting to IoT, it represents new concepts of future shopping behavior.



About the program

The internship program is run by Chicony Electronic. With the theme of "smart city", we built a product with solid UX and technology research from scratch, and presented the prototype in the end.
